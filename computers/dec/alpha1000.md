### AlphaServer 1000
<img src="/img/dec/alpha-badge.jpg" alt="AlphaServer 1000" class="fullwidth"></img>
I received this machine for free because it had a faulty power supply. After
disassembling the power supply, I didn't find any visible damage—just a loose screw. I
reassembled it, and surprisingly, the power supply worked fine.


When I first got the system, I couldn't access the ARC firmware console. Entering "arc"
    at the SRM console would display "ARC firmware loaded" and then freeze. After some
    research, I found a post on Vogons describing a similar issue on an AlphaStation.
    Following the same repair method for the Dallas DS1287 TOY NVRAM resolved the problem.


I currently have OpenVMS 8.4, Tru64 5.1B, Windows NT, and Windows 2000 installed on the
system.
