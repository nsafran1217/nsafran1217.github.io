# Digital Equipment Corporation
*Last Updated: 02-May-2025*

<img src="/img/dec/dec-logo.png" alt="DEC Logo" class="inline" width="200px"></img>
Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC) fascinates me because of its significant impact on
the computing world, yet most people outside of tech have never heard of it.

By the time I started using computers, DEC was already out of business, so I never had the
chance to experience their machines in everyday life.
