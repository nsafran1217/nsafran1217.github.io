Nixie and VFD projects

Like many others, I am fascinated by old display technologies, especially those from the Soviet Union. I've completed a few projects using old neon, Nixie, and VFD displays. I also have some other interesting display tubes sitting on the shelf, such as thyratrons and EL displays. When I have time, I plan to build a clock featuring six different numeric displays.
