Z80 Clock Board

This board just controls the clock and reset signal for the computer. I do not have a reset circuit when the computer is turned on, so the computer needs to be manually reset. You can halt the computer with a switch and manually step the processor with a button on the front.
The board design is based on Ben Eater's design for his 6502 computer.

The schematic is not complete.
