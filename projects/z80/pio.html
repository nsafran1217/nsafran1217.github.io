Z80 PIO Board

I wanted to try out wire wrap, but I didn't have enough space on the back of the board, or any wire wrap sockets. So, I soldered pin headers on either side of the ICs and wire wrapped on the top of the board. Its not the nicest looking wire wrap, but I'm happy with how it came out. It's also easy than doing point to point soldering in my opinion.Â
