SPARCStation 10

Sun Microsystems Home

Overview

This is a pizza box form factor workstation. My example is pretty low spec with 48MB of memory, 40MHz CPU, and 2.1 GB hard disk. The NVRAM in this system was replaced with a NOS part.

I don't use this computer much. I believe SunOS 4.0.x is installed on the disk. I would eventually like to get another CPU, or even a Hyper SPARC if one comes up for a good price.
