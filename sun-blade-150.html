Sun Blade 150

Sun Microsystems Home

Overview

The Sun Blade 150 marks the end of Sun's proprietary keyboard interface, and the 13W3 video connector. The keyboard on the Sun Blade workstations are USB, though they still have the standard Sun layout. I haven't tested a standard PC keyboard on this yet.
This Sun Blade 150 has a 650 MHz UltraSPARC IIe with 256MB of RAM. I haven't messed with this computer much yet, so I'm not sure of the disk size. The original CDROM drive was bad, but I still have it. I should be able to find a CDROM drive that has the same faceplate, so I can swap the gray faceplate over and make the front look original again.
